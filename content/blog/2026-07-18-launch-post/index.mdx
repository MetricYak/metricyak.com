Metrics dashboards tell you what changed and then leave you to go through it all and find out exactly where and why. Then what happens after? More clicks and calls.

We want to create something that aims to remove that boring long process, and better yet make it available for everyone through open-source.

## So what is the goal of MetricYak?

A complete stack that let's you define and explore metrics, create monitors and kick off investigations and workflows. We will also let you query our open book APIs to power your own stack. *(It's your data anyway)*


We're just getting started and are constantly building. 

Follow along in our [github](https://www.github.com/metricyak/metricyak) and even try it yourself, raise issues and help us out.

Or just go to our [docs](/docs/getting-started/).
